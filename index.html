Gulab Jamun

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for syrup)

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, and water to form a smooth dough.
  2. Shape into small balls and fry until golden brown.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup and soak the fried balls in it for 2 hours.

Poha

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup flattened rice (poha)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup peanuts

Recipe

  1. Rinse poha under water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Sauté onions and peanuts in oil, then add turmeric and salt.
  3. Mix in poha and cook for 2 minutes. Serve hot.

Sandwich

Ingrediants

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/4 cup chopped vegetables (tomato, cucumber, onion)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise

Recipe

  1. Spread mayonnaise on the bread slices.
  2. Layer the vegetables between the bread slices.
  3. Grill or serve as is.
